Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe you won't want to miss out. It comes from my grandmother who lives in Italian. It is a classic recipe with a modern twist.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella
  • parmesan

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring a pot of water to boiling
  3. Shred the mozzarella
  4. Grate the parmesan
  5. Assemble the lasagna and place into the oven
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before cutting
